Atik Asif Khan Akash

My name is Md Atik Asif Khan Akash. I am currently a Lecturer (2023–present) at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Daffodil International University, where I have the privilege of contributing to teaching, research, and academic development. I previously received my Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Daffodil International University in August 2021. During my undergraduate years, I was fortunate to work under the guidance of esteemed researchers at the Health Informatics Research Laboratory (HIRL) and the Multi-Disciplinary Action Research Lab (MARS), contributing to impactful projects and publications.
